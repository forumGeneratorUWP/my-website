Forums

Using our platform, you can create structured forums with categories, threads, and posts tailored to your audience. we are FORUM GEN!

In 2021, two college roommates — Maya, a passionate UX designer, and Leo, a backend wizard — were fed up with how clunky and outdated most online forum tools were. They noticed that while social media had evolved, forums had stagnated: bloated with ads, hard to customize, and impossible to moderate cleanly. During a late-night brainstorming session in their dorm, Maya joked, “We should just generate our own forums from scratch.” Leo paused, opened his laptop, and replied, “Why not?”

Over the next few months, they built a prototype that allowed users to create fully functional, modern forums in under five minutes. It featured drag-and-drop layouts, plug-and-play moderation tools, and AI-assisted community growth tips. They called it Forum Gen — short for Forum Generator — and posted a demo on Reddit. Within 48 hours, it exploded. Indie game developers, neighborhood organizers, and even niche hobby groups started asking for beta access.

What started as a joke between friends became a platform powering thousands of online communities. Forum Gen grew into a company driven by one simple mission: put the power of digital gathering back in the hands of everyday people.
